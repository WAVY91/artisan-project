Makeup Artist

Makeup Artistry

Makeup artistry is a hot cake for ladies (and guys too, no cap). Weddings, birthdays, and photo shoots keep artists busy, making it one of the best handworks for young Nigerians.

Charge N5,000 to N20,000 per face, and you could pocket N80,000 to N150,000 monthly as a side hustle. It’s all about enhancing beauty, and Nigerians love to look good.

Brushes, foundation, and other products cost N20,000 to N50,000 to start. Learn in 3 to 6 months at a beauty school or via YouTube, and you’re set. This is one of the most profitable skills in Nigeria, especially in places like Lagos where events plenty.

TPost your work on Instagram, and watch bookings roll in. It’s a vocational skill in Nigeria that pays fast if you’re good with blending and brows.
